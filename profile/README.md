# 🌊 Unda Rubra · 红色电波

**Unda Rubra**（拉丁语：红色电波）是学生社群 **XDUCraft** 的 GitHub 组织。  
这里承载着我们对 Minecraft 的热爱，也凝聚着西电（Xidian University）的电子信息底蕴。

**Unda Rubra** (Latin: *Red Wave*) is the GitHub organization of the student community **XDUCraft**.  
It embodies our passion for Minecraft and carries forward the electronic information excellence of Xidian University.

---

## 🎯 关于我们 / About Us

XDUCraft 成立于西安电子科技大学，致力于为全校师生打造一个开放、有趣的 Minecraft 交流平台。  
我们不仅是游戏玩家，更是校园文化的传播者——在方块世界中复刻西电地标、组织校内外活动，用创意展现学校风采。

XDUCraft was founded at Xidian University, aiming to build an open and engaging Minecraft platform for all faculty and students.  
We are not just players, but also ambassadors of campus culture—recreating Xidian landmarks in blocks, organizing events, and showcasing our school’s spirit through creativity.

组织名称 **Unda Rubra** 取自“红色电波”之意：
- **Unda**（波）—— 既是电磁波，也象征信息的流动，呼应学校在通信与电子领域的深厚积淀；
- **Rubra**（红色）—— 灵感来源于 Minecraft 中的红石（Redstone），代表着电路、逻辑与创造力，寓意社团在技术与创意上的探索精神。

The name **Unda Rubra** derives from the idea of “Red Wave”:
- **Unda** — wave, representing electromagnetic waves and the flow of information, reflecting Xidian’s deep roots in communications and electronics;
- **Rubra** — red, inspired by Minecraft’s redstone, symbolizing circuitry, logic, and creativity—embodying the club’s spirit of exploration in both technology and imagination.

---

## 🛠️ 为什么建立这个组织？ / Why This Organization?

社团的日常运营与项目开发需要技术支撑。  
我们将所有与服务器运维、模组开发、插件配置、建筑存档相关的代码与资源集中托管于此，便于协作与管理，也希望能为西电的开源文化贡献一份力量。

The daily operation and project development of the club require technical support.  
We centralize all code and resources related to server maintenance, mod development, plugin configuration, and building archives here, making collaboration and management easier while contributing to Xidian’s open‑source culture.
